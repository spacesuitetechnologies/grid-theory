Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information Grid Theory ("we") handles when you use gridtheory.io (the "Site"). We keep data collection to a minimum and do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Handle

  • Wallet addresses you enter. When you use the eligibility / verify tools, the address you paste is used to read public blockchain data. We do not need your name, email, or password to do this.
  • On-chain data. NFT holdings and transactions are already public on the Ethereum blockchain; we simply read them.
  • Contact details. If you email us, we receive whatever you choose to send (e.g., your email address and message).
  • Basic usage data. Like most sites, our hosting and any analytics may log standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed.

3. The Blockchain Is Public

Ethereum is a public ledger. Wallet addresses, balances, and transactions are visible to anyone and are outside our control. Connecting a wallet or entering an address does not make that information private.

4. How We Use Information

  • to provide features such as holder verification and the digital vault;
  • to operate, maintain, and improve the Site;
  • to respond to your inquiries and provide support;
  • to protect against fraud, abuse, and security issues.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

The Site may use your browser's local storage for basic preferences and may use privacy- friendly analytics. We do not use cookies to build advertising profiles.

6. Third-Party Services

We rely on third parties to operate the Site, including Ethereum RPC and indexing providers, decentralized storage gateways (IPFS), hosting (Vercel), and marketplaces (such as OpenSea). When you interact with these services, their own privacy policies apply.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law. Addresses entered for an eligibility check are processed in your browser and are not stored by us as a personal profile.

8. Your Choices & Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

9. Children

The Site is not intended for anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Never share your seed phrase or private keys with anyone — including us.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

12. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email admin@spacesuite.io or reach us on @gridtheoryhq.
